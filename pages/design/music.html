design

this is a jewelry box that I made as part of a team. when you wave your hand in front of it, the lid and the drawer both open to reveal what is inside. another wave of the hand closes the box.

process

we first made this box out of cardboard, held together with tape. the ballerina's hand was attached to a piece of string which allowed her to open and close the box. from there, we made an iteration in wood, but it didn't have the finish that we wanted. we finally purchased white acrylic and used that for the exterior of the box. the arduino components stayed the same between iterations.

materials & software

laser cutter, acrylic, acrylic adhesive, arduino board & circuits, c
