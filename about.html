About

Nice to meet you!

I'm a 19-year-old student at Brown using my coursework in applied math and computer science to explore and learn how to unpack complex problems to find elegant solutions.

This past summer, I worked as a teaching assistant at Girls Who Code at Moody's in New York City. During a 7-week long intensive summer program, I taught 20 high schoolers the fundamentals of computer science through a series of lessons in Python, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, and robotics.

In my free time, I like to explore by going to concerts, surfing, reading, and trying new places to eat. I'm also a TA for various computer science courses and an organizer for Hack@Brown.

I'm always interested in crazy new opportunities, and I'd love to hear from you!
